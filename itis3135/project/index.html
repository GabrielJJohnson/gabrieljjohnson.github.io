Welcome to the Bible Study Program

Join a guided, trackable study of the New Testament with built-in checklists, schedules, and resources.

What you'll find here

  • Guided reading plans with progress checkboxes
  • Course summaries and learning goals
  • Interactive schedule and checklist to keep you on track
  • Contact & slideshow of course staff

This program is designed to help readers deepen their knowledge of the New Testament using short readings, guided questions, and a simple tracking interface.

Anyone who wants a structured, gentle approach to reading and studying the New Testament: students, church groups, or independent readers.
