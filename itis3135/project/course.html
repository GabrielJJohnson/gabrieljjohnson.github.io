Course Summary

This course offers a guided reading of the New Testament in a timeframe chosen by the learner. Each module includes readings, reflection questions, and short summaries.

Module 1: Gospels Overview

Focus on Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John — setting, key themes, and starter questions.

Module 2: Acts & Early Church

Study of Acts and the development of the early Christian movement.

Module 3: Epistles

Selected Pauline and General Epistles — theological themes and practical application.
