Course Summary

This course offers a guided reading of the New Testament in a year's span. Below is a list of the four course modules and a short description of each.

Module One: The Gospels

Focus on the firsthand accounts of the life of Jesus of Nazareth, the Way, the Truth, and the Life.

  • Matthew
  • Mark
  • Luke
  • John

Module Two: Acts & Early Church

Study of Acts and the foundation of the Early Church, along with information on the Early Church Fathers.

  • Acts

Module Three: Pauline Epistles

Letters written by St. Paul to specific people or churches, meant to provide theological guidance.

  • Romans
  • 1 Corinthians
  • 2 Corinthians
  • Galatians
  • Ephesians
  • Philippians
  • Colossians
  • 1 Thessalonians
  • 2 Thessalonians
  • 1 Timothy
  • 2 Timothy
  • Titus
  • Philemon

Module Four: General Epistles

Letters written by apostles to wider audiences rather than specific individuals.

  • Hebrews
  • James
  • 1 Peter
  • 2 Peter
  • 1 John
  • 2 John
  • 3 John
  • Jude

Module Five: Prophetic Literature

The Revelation of John is an account full of imagery and literal meaning. It is a difficult book to tackle, so some further guidance may be required.

  • Revelation
