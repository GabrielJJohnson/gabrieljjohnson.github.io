Course Summary

This course offers a guided reading of the New Testament in a timeframe chosen by the learner. Each module includes readings, reflection questions, and short summaries.

Module 1: The Gospels

Focus on Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John.

Module 2: Acts & Early Church

Study of Acts and the foundation of the Early Church, along with information on the Early Church Fathers.

Module 3: Letters and Prophetic Literature

Letters establishing core beliefs of The Way and prophetic literature given in the Revelation of John.
