Bible Study Project Overview

Website Plan

  • The goal of the Website is to allow access to a bible study program to help keep track of readings, learning, and studying.
  • This website is intended for anyone wishing to expand their intellect on the Bible and have a seemless place to do so
  • The website will have a guide/study plan for the New Testament, as well as check boxes to keep track of the process

Page Design

Home

  • Purpose: Welcoming introduction page
  • Audience: Everyone
  • Content: Overview page and home area
  • Interactivity: Collapsible information for the website

About

  • Purpose: Resource to learn more about goals and services
  • Audience: Those who would like more information on the goals of the course/site and what to expect
  • Content: More information about goals and expectations
  • Interactivity: Collapsible information for the website

Course Summary

  • Purpose: Access to course description and its overview
  • Audience: Those who want an overview of the course
  • Content: Summary of the course and timeframe

Schedule

  • Purpose: Clear and readable location for tracking progress
  • Audience: Those who want to see the outlined schedule for studying
  • Content: Schedule for the course and checklist to track progress
  • Interactivity: Calendar adjacent checklist to track progress

Contact

  • Purpose: Gives visitors a place to inquire about the resources provided
  • Audience: Those who may need to inquire further
  • Content: Contact information for Luke, my client, and myself
  • Interactivity: Image slideshow for Luke, my client
