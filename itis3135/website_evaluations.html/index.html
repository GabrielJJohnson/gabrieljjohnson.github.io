Website Evaluations

Good Website: Firewatch Game

Description: This website advertises a game, Firewatch. The site offers information, download options, and trailers/content for the game.

  • Contrast:
    • Strong contrast between text and background
    • Text color and background color oppose each other
  • Repetition:
    • Fonts are consistent
    • Repetition is built from the beginning, so it establishes the page going forward
  • Alignment:
    • Alignment is consistent, whether that is on the left or centered
    • Margins and padding are balanced
  • Proximity:
    • Proximity is logical
    • Related elements are grouped together, nothing feels out of place, etc.

Assessment: This website is a very good example of what one should attempt to reasonably obtain

Bad Website: The Big Ugly Website

Description: This site is made for the purpose of looking bad. I found it resembles our activity meant to purposefully make a CRAPPY (and crappy) website.

  • Contrast:
    • Colors clash together
    • Bright and mismatched
  • Repetition:
    • No consistency in fonts or their reason for being used
    • Poster child for "ugly" detailing
  • Alignment:
    • Alignment is uneven and disorganized
    • No consistency
  • Proximity:
    • Too close or too far apart
    • Everything feels out of place on the site

Assessment: This site is the pinnacle of a bad website. It is disorganized, inconsistent, and holds no attempt to be pleasing to the viewer.
